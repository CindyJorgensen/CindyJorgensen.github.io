Temple Inn & Suites provides all the services you need in one place.

We offer the following Specialized Services to temple patrons.

  • Access to changing rooms and bathroom facilities to those who have traveled far and need to change clothing prior to their visit to the temple.
  • We offer a low-cost playroom for short-term babysitting services for children of patrons.
  • Long-term stay accomodations in kitchenette suites for full-time temple missionaries.
  • Multiple wedding receptions options that can easily be customized and a simple catering menu and kitchen access for food preparation and serving.
  • A famly serach center that supports family search and ordinance work.

Our Guests enjoy the following free amenities:

  • Continental Breakfast Buffet
  • Exercise/ Workout Room
  • Indoor Pool
  • WiFi high speed internet access and smart TVs
  • Parking
  • Temple shuttle
  • Family history consultation and family ordinance card services.
